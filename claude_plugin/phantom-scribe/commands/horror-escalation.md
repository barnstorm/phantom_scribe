---
description: Build a Dread Ladder escalation plan for horror
---

Use the `horror-escalation` agent.

Ask for:
1) Thumbnail + horror tone
2) Target length (chapters or audio hours)
3) Threat concept (even vague)
4) Rule hardness
5) Content limits (no gore, etc.)

Output:
- Threat rule card
- Dread ladder stages (Itch → Aftermath)
- Chapter escalation grid
- 3–6 set-piece seeds
- Canon/timeline follow-ups

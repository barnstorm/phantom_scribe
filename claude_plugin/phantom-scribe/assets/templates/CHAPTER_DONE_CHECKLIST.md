# Chapter Done Checklist (Phantom Scribe)

Use this immediately after drafting or heavily revising a chapter.

## Coverage
- [ ] Beats covered match the plan (no missing or double-covered beats).
- [ ] Timeline and locations align with surrounding chapters.
- [ ] Character knowledge/emotional state is consistent with what came before.

## Momentum
- [ ] The chapter answers at least one question.
- [ ] The chapter introduces a new constraint/twist/cost.
- [ ] The chapter ends with motion (hook).

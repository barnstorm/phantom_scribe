# BEAT SHEET (Template)

Use this to sketch the spine from thumbnail → outline → chapters. Adjust beats/act count to fit genre.

## Act 1 – Setup (Inciting Incident)
- **Hook:** _Opening image/situation_
- **Inciting Incident:** _Event that disrupts status quo_
- **Turn 1:** _Protagonist pulled in/commits_
- **Key Stakes/Questions:** _What’s at risk? What do they want?_

## Act 2 – Escalation (Complications & Midpoint)
- **Early Complications:** _Obstacles, allies/antagonists introduced_
- **First Threshold:** _Point of no return_
- **Midpoint (Reversal/Reveal):** _New information or shift in goal_
- **Cost:** _What it costs them to continue_

## Act 3 – Crisis (All Is Lost → Resolve)
- **Crisis/“Dark Night”:** _Lowest point; plan fails or stakes peak_
- **Decision:** _Choice that defines the climax_
- **Climax:** _Confrontation/solution; protagonist acts_

## Act 4 – Fallout/Resolution (Epilogue optional)
- **Resolution:** _Immediate outcome; loose ends tied or left intentionally open_
- **Aftermath/Change:** _How the protagonist/world is different_
- **Epilogue (optional):** _Echo/theme reinforcement_

## Beat-to-Chapter Mapping (Template)
| Beats | Chapter | Notes |

| 1-2   | Ch 1    | Setup/hook |
| 3-4   | Ch 2    | Inciting/Turn |
| ...   | ...     | ... |

## Notes
- Keep beats small and specific; aim for 1–3 beats per chapter.
- Note POV, time/location shifts, and knowledge gating per beat.
- Revisit beats after drafting to ensure coverage before editing passes.

# Harmon × Crouch Chapter Grids (Templates)

These grids combine:
- Macro structure: `HARMON_STORY_CIRCLE_TEMPLATE.md`
- Chapter rhythm: `CROUCH_PACING_SHEET_TEMPLATE.md`

Use one grid, fill it, then mirror the beat ranges into your beat tracker/TODOs.

---

## Quick How-To
1. Fill the **Circle Beat** sections first (macro intent).
2. Fill **Chapter Question** and **New Constraint** for every chapter (pacing).
3. Only then assign **Beats** (coverage control).
4. Mirror filenames and status into your TODOs and beat tracker.
